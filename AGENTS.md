## Mandatory bilingual parity / Paridad bilingüe obligatoria

## Funnel and sales messaging rule

Encore Bio Labs uses two intentionally different messaging levels. Determine which environment is being edited before writing or revising copy.

**Project classification:** This alternate Encore Bio Labs site is the **main aggressive conversion site**. Apply the Main Encore Bio Labs website rules below to its landing pages, catalog, product pages, kits, intake pathways, and conversion content. Do not soften this site into pre-lander messaging. The pre-landing rules apply only to separately identified advertising pre-lander projects or pages.

### Pre-landing pages

Pre-landers must be calm, educational, credible, research-focused, policy-conscious, and useful as standalone resources. Their purpose is to explain a research topic, build trust, qualify interest, and link naturally to the most relevant main-site category or product page.

- Use neutral topic-led headlines and educational calls to action.
- Provide meaningful original content, limitations, responsible context, FAQs, Research Use Only disclosure, and policy/footer links.
- Use transparent, functional, relevant links without automatic or deceptive redirects.
- Do not use aggressive direct-response tactics, purchase pressure, prominent pricing, unsupported claims, guaranteed outcomes, transformation promises, medical recommendations, dosing/reconstitution/injection instructions, fake endorsements, false scarcity, or fabricated statistics.
- Design to maximize the probability of Google Ads compliance, but never state or imply that approval is guaranteed.
- When conversion pressure conflicts with advertising-policy safety, policy-conscious language wins.

Preferred pre-lander CTAs include “Explore the Research Category,” “View Available Research Compounds,” “Continue to the Research Catalog,” and “Review Available Kit Options.”

### Main Encore Bio Labs website

The main site is the primary conversion destination. Its messaging should be confident, direct, benefit-led, persuasive, easy to understand, and conversion-focused.

- Clearly present products, pricing, available options, Complete Kit advantages, convenience, service, trust indicators, shipping/delivery information, objection handling, and repeated conversion paths.
- Prefer specific CTAs such as “Shop the Collection,” “Choose Your Kit,” “View Products and Pricing,” “Explore Best Sellers,” “Start Your Research,” and “Contact Encore Bio Labs.”
- Remove vague, repetitive, or unnecessarily technical material that distracts from product selection or conversion.
- Aggressive marketing must remain accurate. Never guarantee outcomes; fabricate testimonials, certifications, studies, or statistics; misrepresent regulatory status; imply medical treatment; use fake scarcity; or mislead about pricing, availability, shipping, or kit contents.
- Build differentiation around verifiable product information, Complete Kit convenience, service quality, clear pricing, and accurate research interests.

### Linking and visual separation

- Link each pre-lander to its most relevant main-site category or product destination rather than defaulting every page to the homepage.
- Pre-landers use calm premium visuals, generous spacing, limited navigation, softer CTAs, clear disclosures, and fast mobile performance.
- The main site uses strong product imagery, prominent choices and pricing, high-contrast CTAs, energetic hierarchy, clear kit presentation, and persistent access to search, intake, catalog, and contact.
- Both environments must remain recognizably part of the Encore Bio Labs brand.
